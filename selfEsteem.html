LOW SELF ESTEEM

Tips on how to better yourself and boost your confidence

There are many teens right now who are suffering from low self esteem, and many people fail to realize how much, low self esteem can really effect daily activities, how you personaly feel about yourself, and your life in general. Especially in this generation with all of the social media platforms. Low Self esteem is a big problem that we feel we should adress the topic, while giving helpful advise on how to overcome self esteem and create confidence in yourself that you deserve.

Rule number one

Accept yourself for you are! You are crafted and created to be the beautiful person that you are; you don't need anyones approval and you need to realize that you are PERFECT the way you are. If you can't love yourself for the way you naturally and truly are how do you expect someone else to be. You have to be comfortable in your own skin, do what makes YOU feel good, be happy, don't stress over the little things, You are amazing and once you realize that, your self esteem will increase by alot!

Rule number two

Avoid people who make you feel bad about yourself. Negative energy can always lower your self esteem. Being put into situations that you don't want to be put in can always make yourslef feel toxic and LOWER your self esteem. So, removing negative enrgy, friends, family, and anything that makes you feel bad you should remove them from your life. You should always surrond yourself with others who make you feel good about yourself.

Rule number Three

You have to REALLY belive the affremations you tell yourself. It's like lying to yourself. You can say something all you want until you truly believe it. If you don't really believe what you tell yourself you will never truly believe in yourself nor are you helping yourslef boost your self esteem.
