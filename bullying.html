Bullying

What Is Bullying?

In order to prevent bullying, one must first know what bullying is. Bullying is the act of seeking attention and satisfaction by harming others who they view as vulnerable and easy targets. Bullies are people that have strained relationships with their peers and family. They don't really understand other's feelings and most commonly, misread their intentions.

How To Prevent Bullying/Being Bullied:

A few ways to help prevent bullying is by:

1) Help Kids Understand What Bullying Is - Talk about what bullying is, encourage them to talk to an adult.

2) Communicate With Kids - Make sure to talk to kids; ask them how they are doing, listen to them, and understand their concerns.



A few ways to prevent being bullied is by:



1) Walk Away From The Bully - Bullies often get satisfaction from hurtings others. Getting some sort of reaction out of a person will just encourage the bully to continue with their bullying. By walking away, your body language will show that you don't care.

2) Walk With A Buddy - Have a friends groups to talk and hang out in.

3) Stand Up For Yourself - Bullies tend to pick on people that don't stick up for themselves. Telling the bully enough is enough, will show the bully to back off.
