F A M I L Y

Many define Family as a group that consists of parents and kids living together, most commonly through a blood relation. Although this may be, family isn't always defined by a blood relation. Family is consisted of people that you can trust and confide in; that support, respect, and love you no matter what. The relationship with your family can either make you or break you.

Pros:

1) Support System - A family is your biggest support system. They may not always agree with your choices, but they will always support your decisions no matter what.

2) Knowledge - Families always tend to give wise advice when we need it the most. They have more experience and would not lead you in the wrong direction.

3) Love - Most of the time, families, especially parents, love their kids a lot.

Cons:

1) Pressure - Families tend to pressure kids into doing things they don't want to do in order to fit into society and be accepted by their own people.

2) Making decisions for you - Parents have a tendency to make decsions for their kids. If kids don't get to make decisions for themselves, it will be harder for them to make in future due to the lack of practice.

3) Having a favorite child- No matter what parents say, they will always have a favorite child. Having a favorite child will hit on a child's self-esteem. It will also cause damage on family unit.
