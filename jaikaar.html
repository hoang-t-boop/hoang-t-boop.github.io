Hello! My name is Jaikaar Kaur. I live in the Bay Area. I am a huge Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, and The Hobbit fan. I love vegan food, Indian food, pasta, and CHEX MIX!! Some of my favorite things to do is read, eat food, sleep, exercise, and watch crime shows. During the summer of 2019, I participated in a seven week program, known as the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. I, along with 21 other girls, was given the opportunity to learn how to code and create a strong sisterhood bond with each other through computer science (coding) and fun activities.
