👒 FASHION 👒

VIC'S PICK

She keeps her style very open. She likes to have very many different outfits to choose from, her favorite look is the street-look. She doesn't like to dress up unless its a sundress, she doesn't really like puffy dresses at all. She likes to accessorize herself all the time. Her biggest advice when it comes to fashion is, "be yourself and don't look for designer products cause it's all the same stuff."

GLO'S PICK

Glo likes to keep it very simple and comfy, you can see her in sweats almost every single day. One advice that she gives to everyone is, "Comfort over anything." But if she had an event to go to, she'd probably be there in a trench coat as she believes that trench coats go with any outfit and any event out there.

LEE'S PICK

Lee likes to keep it simple but chic everytime. She really likes Chanel becuase their logo looks really cute and all their handbags and perfume are on a whole another level. She also loves wearing ripped jeans with graphic t-shirts. She also prefers to wear those sundresses that are perfect for literally any ocassion there is and also because they are asthetically pleasing. And finally, sunglasses are the cherry on top as she says, "Sunglasses just complete the outfit."

But along with that she also mentioned to "be you and wear whatever works out for you."

TAN'S PICK

She likes to dress very comfortable but cute. Some of Tan's favorite places to shop are at Pacsun, Urban Outfitters, Abercrombie and Fitch, and Uni Qlo because of the comfy outfits that they offer. She also likes nike shoes and shorts to increase the comfort level.
